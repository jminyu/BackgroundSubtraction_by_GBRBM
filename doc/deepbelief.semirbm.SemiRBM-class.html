Known Subclasses:

An implementation of the semi-restricted Boltzmann machine. In contrast to the RBM, the SemiRBM can also have lateral connections between visible units.

References:

  • Osindero, S. and Hinton, G.E. (2008). Modeling image patches with a directed hiarchy of Markov random fields.
